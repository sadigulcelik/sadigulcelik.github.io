Rules

The game of Rock Paper Scissors is based on predicting the opponent's move, and acting accordingly.

Each player must:

  • Choose either rock, paper or scissors.
  • Submit their response.
  • Hope for victory.

One wins when one picks

  • Rock when their opponent picks scissors
  • Scissors when their opponent picks paper
  • Paper when their opponent picks rock

One loses when one picks

  • Rock when their opponent picks paper
  • Paper when their opponent picks scissors
  • Scissors when their opponent picks rock
